Rice Krispie Treats

My favorite treat!

Rice Krispies Treats are a confection commonly made through binding Kellogg's Rice Krispies or another crisp rice cereal together with butter or margarine and marshmallow. Though they are traditionally home-made, Kellogg's began to market the treats themselves in 1995.

Ingredients

  • Rice Krispie Cereal
  • Marshmallow
  • Salted Butter
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Line a baking dish with parchment paper
  2. In large sauce pan melt butter over medium heat. Once melted add in marshmallows. Continue stirring until melted
  3. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla. Add cereal and one additional cup of marshmallows. Gently stir until combined.
  4. Pour into baking dish. Press gently with finger tips.
  5. Allow to set for 1 hour. Cut and enjoy!
